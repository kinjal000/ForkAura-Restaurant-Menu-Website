About ForkAura

Our Story

ForkAura began with a dream to blend authentic recipes with contemporary culinary trends. Established in 2018, we now serve a vibrant community of food lovers who value taste and quality.

Meet the Owner

Chef Aarav Mehta, the visionary behind ForkAura, brings 20 years of global culinary experience. His philosophy: “Food is an emotion, and every dish should speak to the soul.”

Our Chefs

Our talented chefs are handpicked from top culinary schools and Michelin-star kitchens. They bring innovation to every plate while honoring age-old flavors and techniques.

Philosophy & Values

We believe in fresh ingredients, sustainability, and community. Our commitment goes beyond taste—we aim to make dining a heartwarming experience, every single time.

Ambiance & Experience

The design of ForkAura blends rustic charm with modern luxury. Whether it’s a romantic dinner or a family feast, the atmosphere is always warm and welcoming.

Looking Ahead

With plans to expand across cities and even launch our own line of organic spices, ForkAura is more than a restaurant—it’s a movement redefining Indian dining culture.
